The Art of Commanding AI.

Learn how to direct AI, so your imagination can become reality.

The Anatomy of a Perfect Prompt

Think of AI as a very smart intern who knows nothing yet. Be specific. Break every request into 4 pillars.

01. Role / Persona

"Act as a senior web designer who builds beautiful, modern interfaces."

02. Business Context

"I'm building a personal expense tracker for my own use — minimalist style."

03. Specific Task

"Create a form that lets me log an expense: name, amount, and category."

04. Output Rules

"Use a dark background. Show a running total at the top. Mobile-friendly."

Prompt Examples: Bad vs. Good

❌ Too Vague

"Make me a cool expense app."

  • No context about design style.
  • "Cool" means nothing to AI.
  • AI will guess — and probably guess wrong.

✅ Vibe Coder Style

"Act as an expert app builder. Create a dark-themed expense tracker. It should have a form at the top (expense name, amount, category dropdown), and a list below showing all entries with a running total. Save data to local storage so it persists on refresh."

Steering the Wheel (Iteration)

Vibe coding is a back-and-forth conversation with a creative partner — not a one-shot magic trick.

1. Build the Foundation

Ask AI to create the skeleton. Don't ask for everything at once.

2. Course Correct

Something off? Just say so. "Move the total to the top. Change the button to blue."

3. Polish to Perfection

Add micro-details that make it feel premium — hover effects, smooth transitions, icons.
